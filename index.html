FAQ

Questions? Answered

What is TAARI Gala Night?
TAARI Gala Night is an annual celebration of African culture, creativity, and storytelling. The African Experience brings together art, music, fashion, and cuisine to honor tradition, identity, and expression.
What's included in the VIP ticket?
VIP tickets include premium front-row seating, exclusive VIP lounge access, priority red carpet photos, complimentary gift bag, plus all the benefits of general admission including dinner, performances, and after party access.
Is there a dress code?
The dress code is formal/black-tie optional. We encourage guests to embrace African-inspired attire to celebrate the theme of The African Experience.
When does early bird pricing end?
Early bird pricing ends on February 15, 2026. After this date, regular pricing of $100 for General Admission and $150 for VIP will apply.
Can I get a refund?
Tickets are non-refundable but are transferable. If you cannot attend, you may transfer your ticket to another person by contacting our team before the event.
